Private Client Checking Account Manual

Monthly Service Fee

This account type offers exclusive benefits and personalized services tailored to meet your financial needs. However, please be aware that there is a monthly service fee associated with maintaining this account.
The monthly service fee of $35 is applied to cover the costs of providing premium services and enhanced features that come with your private client account. These services may include dedicated customer support, access to specialized financial advisors, preferential interest rates on loans and savings accounts, and various other perks designed to streamline your banking experience.
It's important to note that this fee is automatically deducted from your account balance on a monthly basis. You may notice this charge reflected in your account statement as "Private Client Checking Account Service Fee."
